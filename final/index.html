Doctor Breakfast

DOCTOR BREAKFAST

SHOWS MUSIC PODCAST GALLERY

Doctor Breakfast is a producer and rapper from Brooklyn, NY. His beats are a blend of soul and pop melodies supported by a floor of hard hitting hip hop beats. The doc is also the creator and host of his own monthly podcast - The Doctor Breakfast Podcast. He serves up a dish best served cold with his artistry and always gets the crowd moving in any event.
